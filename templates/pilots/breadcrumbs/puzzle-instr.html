Data Visualization Study

Section 2: Image Puzzle Task

In this portion of the study, you will view an interface which contains several parts: a square window showing a portion of a collage of bird images; pictures marked with colorful shapes to find within the collage (or "target pictures"); and two function buttons called "FOUND" and "FINISH".

The main task is to find 18 target pictures within the collage. You can drag within the visualization window to see more of the image collage. There may be a delay before some images appear in the collage.

An example of a target picture is provided below. The target picture has colorful circles in the corners. When you find a target picture, move the target picture to the center of the visualization window, and click on the "FOUND" button.

The target picture will be clearly marked as found (shown in the picture below), and the count of total targets found will increase.

You can end the task and move on to the next page at any time by clicking the "FINISH" button. Once you confirm to finish, you will not be able to interact with the collage anymore.

When you are ready to start, please press "Continue" below.
