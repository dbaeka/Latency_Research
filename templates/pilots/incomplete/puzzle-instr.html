Data Visualization Study

Section 2: Image Puzzle Task

In this portion of the study, you will view an interface which contains several parts: a square window showing a portion of a collage of bird images; a description of a picture to find within the collage (or a "target picture"); and two function buttons called "FOUND" and "FINISH".

There is a single kind of target picture within the collage. The main task is to find the target picture. You can drag or zoom in/out within the visualization window to see more of the image collage. There may be a delay before some images appear in the collage.

When you find the target picture, move the target picture to the center of the visualization window, make sure that the zoom level has been set to the biggest (Level 5), and click on the "FOUND" button. The web page will pop out a window to confirm whether you have completed the task or not. You can stop finding by clicking the "FINISH" button. Once you confirm to finish, you will not be able to interact with the collage anymore.

When you are ready to start, please press "Continue" below.
