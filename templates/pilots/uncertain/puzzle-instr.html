Data Visualization Study

Section 2: Image Puzzle Task

In this portion of the study, you will view an interface which contains several parts: a square window showing a portion of a collage of bird images; a description of a picture to find within the collage (or a "target picture"); and two function buttons called "FOUND" and "FINISH".

There is a single target picture within the collage. The main task is to find this target picture. You can drag within the visualization window to see more of the image collage. There may be a delay before some images appear in the collage.

The target picture is somewhere on the left side of your starting position in the collage. A diagram of the location of the target picture is provided below:

When you find the target picture, move the target picture to the center of the visualization window, and click on the "FOUND" button. The web page will pop out a window to confirm whether you have made progress in the task or not. The target picture will be clearly marked as found after the confirmation. You can stop finding by clicking the "FINISH" button. Once you confirm to finish, you will not be able to interact with the collage anymore.

When you are ready to start, please press "Continue" below.
